WhatsApp Sender

Bulk messenger

Send WhatsApp messages to hundreds of contacts in minutes

WhatsApp Sender connects to your WhatsApp account, reads phone numbers and messages from an Excel file, and sends them for you — with live progress and the option to reuse files or send custom text, photos, and videos.

Open app

How it works

  1. 1
    Link WhatsApp

    Scan the QR code with your phone to connect your account.

  2. 2
    Upload Excel

    Use columns الرقم (phone) and الرساله (message), or English phone / message.

  3. 3
    Send

    Click send and watch delivery progress. Optionally override the message for everyone, or attach photos and videos.

  4. 4
    Reuse later

    Previously uploaded Excel files stay in your library so you can send again without re-uploading.

Excel-driven

One row per contact. Messages come straight from your spreadsheet.

Custom broadcasts

Replace the Excel message with one text for all recipients, plus media.

Arabic-friendly

Arabic filenames and message text display and send correctly.
